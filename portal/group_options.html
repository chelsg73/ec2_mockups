Screening Groups Confirmation

You previously learned more about the following screening groups:

Group 1 - Treatable Conditions

Rare conditions that are treatable, meaning that early treatment can prevent the most serious symptoms.

(2) Optional Group 2:

Rare conditions that have potential treatments, meaning that treatments may not be able to prevent the most serious symptoms or that new treatments are still being studied.

(3) Optional Group 3:

Risk for Type 1 Diabetes, a common childhood disease that is treatable. (Note: Type 1 Diabetes is not the same as type 2 diabetes, which is more common in adults.)

Remember: In order for Early Check to test your baby for a screening group, you must learn more about it. We want parents who choose screening for their baby to know all the facts.

If you would like to sign up your baby for other screening groups, go back to read about these groups.
