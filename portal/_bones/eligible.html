Find out here if your baby can join Early Check.

If you are a mother, you can sign up your baby for Early Check when

  • you are more than 12 weeks pregnant, or
  • you had a baby less than 1 month old (31 days) ago.

If you are the parent or guardian of a newborn but are not the mother or birthing parent, click here to find out how to join.
