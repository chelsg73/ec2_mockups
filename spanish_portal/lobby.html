Learn more about Early Check screening groups

You must learn more about the Early Check screening groups you’d like your baby to be tested for. These screening groups include Treatable Conditions (Group 1), Conditions with Potential Treatments (Group 2), and Risk for Type 1 Diabetes (Group 3).

Early Check automatically tests babies for Treatable Conditions (Group 1). To sign up your baby for Early Check, you must select to learn more about this group.

If you’d like your baby to be tested for Conditions with Potential Treatments (Group 2) or Risk for Type 1 Diabetes (Group 3), you can choose to learn more about these below along with Treatable Conditions. Conditions with Potential Treatments and Risk for Type 1 Diabetes are optional screening groups. This means that you do not have to choose to have your baby tested for these.

Select one of the options below to learn more. Once you’ve learned about the group(s) you’d like your baby tested for, click “Next” below.
